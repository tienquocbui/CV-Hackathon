About Speakly

Your voice deserves to be heard. We make it professional.

Our Mission

Speakly strives to empower individuals to transform their spoken words into professional, well-structured CVs. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to present themselves at their best, regardless of their background or writing skills.

Our Core Values

  • Simplicity: Making technology accessible to everyone.
  • Accuracy: Delivering precise and reliable transcriptions.
  • Inclusivity: Supporting multiple languages for global users.
  • Innovation: Utilizing cutting-edge AI to enhance user experience.
